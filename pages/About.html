CV

Hey, I am Jonas, a Design Student fom Germany
who deeply fell in love with Product Design and creative
problem solving.

As a User Experience Designer with a background in front-end development, I am currently in my 6th term studying Design at the Georg Simon Ohm University in Nuernberg, Germany.

In my second term, I started working at Siemens as a UI/UX Designer (Working Student). Among other things, I collaborated on international HMI (Human Machine Interface) Design Projects, led Design Sprints in Europe, developed a Design System for the HMI Team, worked on projects for Porsche and other DAX Companies and held Figma Trainings for colleagues in China.

Through this experience, I have developed a strong understanding of user-centered design principles and the ability to work in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

During my 5th term my initially assigned project as a UX-Design-Intern at Google got canceled due to market dynamics. So I went out to have my internship at my favourite Design Agency, BuildinAmsterdam(Best Ecommerce Agency 2017, 2018, 2019 & 2021). During this time I worked on a 3D furniture configurator for vitra.com and supported various other projects involving Dior, TheBalvenie, X-Bionic, and BiA.

On the side, I am working as a Freelance UX Designer, where I explore unique digital solutions for clients worldwide. Here, I make use of my background in different mediums to direct, and collaborate on, multidisciplinary digital projects.

Apart from design, you can see me having a good time skiing (sometimes snowboarding) in Austria, lifting weights in the gym, going on vacations with friends, and going out getting some good food. (Note: Good food is the easiest way to make me happy).

I also love nerding about vintage lenses (I own a Leica R set from the 1980s), milestones in tech, and some beautiful apps and websites that I spend way too much time exploring and capturing.

So that's me. (Or at least a part of me.) If you're interested in getting in touch, please don't hesitate to reach out. Have a great day!
