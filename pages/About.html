Hey, I am Jonas, a Design Student fom Germany
who deeply fell in love with Product Design and creative
problem solving.

Currently i am in my 5th term studying Design at the Georg Simon Ohm University in the beautiful Nuernberg [Germany].

In my second term i started working at Siemens as a UI/UX Designer [Working Student]. Among other things i was in charge of international HMI Design Projects, lead Design Sprints in Europe, developed a Design System for the HMI Team and held Figma Trainings for Collegues in China.

On the side i am currently working as a Freelance UI/UX Designer/Art Director where i explore unique digital solutions for clients worldwide. Here I make use of my background in diferent mediums suche as commercials, photography, illustration and CGI to direct and collaborate on multidisciplinary digital projects.

A few month ago i noticed how muche i love developing standards and processes for my work. Thus i started capturing and sharing them via Medium articels. [And am currently working with a developer friend of mine on a UI & UX-Design Docu.]

Apart from design you can see me having a good time skiing [sometimtes snowboarding] in Austria, lifting weights in the gym, going on vacations with friends, and going out getting some good food. [Note: Good food is the easiest way to make me happy].

I also love nerding about vintage lenses [I own a Leica R set from the 1980s], milestones in tech and some beautiful apps and websites that i spend way to muche time exploring and capturing.

I also really enjoy educating myself financially what propably is one of a few left overs from my time studying Economics. [I studyied Economics and Design for two terms simultaneously but decidet to cut Economics to be able to 100% commit on design.]

So thats me. [Or at least a part of me.] Feel free to contact me if you would like to get in touch. Have a great day!
