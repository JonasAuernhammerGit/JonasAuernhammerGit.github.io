Art Direction - Content Production - Digital Design

Building Community Through Dental Care: A Social Media Campaign for Dentally to Attract Talent and Connect with like-minded Patients.

Client:

Dentally

Dentally is redefining the dental experience with a welcoming and comfortable office, and approach to oral health that puts patients first. They are creating a community-based atmosphere where patients are motivated to take care of their oral health out of love for their bodies. The social media campaign showcases their unique approach to dentistry and gives patients a refreshing perspective on the industry.

For the art direction, we opted for a warm minimalist style reminiscent of Greek interior design to give the impression of the serenity and comfort one feels at home.

The Images were captured in collaboration with the photographer Lerot' [Leon Greiner].

The created content was a carefully curated combination of individual shots of team members, group photography featuring multiple people, imagery of dental equipment, and informative written materials. The goal of this strategic mix was to provide a comprehensive and engaging representation of the dental practice.
