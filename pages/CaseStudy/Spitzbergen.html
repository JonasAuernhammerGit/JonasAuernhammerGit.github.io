UI/UX Design - Art Direction

The project was part of a crucial digitalisation-project for a renowned research institution in Spitzbergen (Norway).
We proposed and implemented a digital certification program that effectively qualified the researchers and improved the selection process.

Client:

SIOS

The project was a component of a larger digitalization initiative for a well-known research institution located in Spitzbergen. My task was to devise a solution for assessing the knowledge of the participating researchers prior to inviting them on an expedition. The solution we proposed was a Quiz App that incorporated elements of gamification. Throughout the entire process, I closely collaborated with the developers and the lead of the overarching digitalization project.

We streamlined the architecture of the web app to make it more easily maintainable.

We incorporated an element of gamification into the experience by providing instant positive feedback upon correctly answering a question.

We successfully created a quiz app that effectively and securely evaluates the knowledge of participating researchers before inviting them on an expedition. The quiz takes less than 10 minutes to complete and provides a seamless experience that was well-received by both the client and the researchers. The digitalization of the process was gladly welcomed as it greatly reduced the need for paperwork.

We were tasked with constructing a custom backend for the client, so they would be fully independent moving forward. In collaboration with the client's internal security and development team, the software was then successfully implemented and hosted on their local servers.
