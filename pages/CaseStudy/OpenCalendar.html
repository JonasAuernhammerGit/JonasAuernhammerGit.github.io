Open Calendar: Elevating Time Management for the Digital Native. An insight into the ongoing Project

Type:

Open Source

The Open Calendar Project is an ongoing open-source initiative that a developer friend and I have initiated. Through the Calendar App, we aim to investigate ways to enhance time management for members of Generation Z. We will document and share all the insights we gain during the design, development, and user testing phases through Medium articles. The entire project, including all assets, will also be published and made available as open-source.

77% of Gen Z believe it's important for businesses to customize interactions, while 76% are looking for companies to send them digital communications they can customize based on their preferences.

To make the user interface as clear as possible, we are exploring ways to use a limited number of UI elements and to transform them organically during interactions. We use targeted continuity of elements so that user always knows where they are.
